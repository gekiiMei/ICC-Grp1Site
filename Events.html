< I am a Coder/ > Series

Sometimes you feel anxious as a student that maybe you’re not doing well in programming or currently you’re having a hard time catching up to the difficulty of the major subjects. Well, you’re not alone. Furthermore, PLM Computer Science Society would be grateful to extend a hand of help. To be an efficient coder or programmer, you should have proficiency in different programming languages, flexible problem-solving skills, and innovative and creative mind. <I am a Coder/> is a 7-week long series of posts designed to equip Computer Science students with knowledge and skills that will surely be helpful to their journey through this course.

About Us

We are the Computer Science Society an organization within Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila (PLM) that serves as both beacon and haven for Computer Science Students. We assist and guide students with the most recent updates and events happening inside the campus. Not only that, but we also find ways and open doors that serves as oppurtunities for aspiring students with discernible talent and passion. Together with our team we'll ensure to help you to have a pleasant college experience in PLM.
